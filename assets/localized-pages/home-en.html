About the game

Wordbox is a free mobile game that contains a bunch of letter games to play on the go. Meet "Pwet Pwet the Lil Beaver", the game's host that welcomes you and teaches you each of the game's rules. He can even play with you as a bot in some of the game modes!

Missions

In the "Missions Menu", you will find random missions to complete by playing game modes.

Some of the missions changes each days, while some for other you have a full week to complete them. And you also have the all-time quests that you can complete at your own pace.

For each completed mission, you earn some money that you can spend in the shop for getting new customization elements!

Will you be able to complete them all?
